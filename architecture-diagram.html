Confidence and Evaluation Intent

The system is intentionally conservative. It separates data quality from policy grounding so weak upstream evidence cannot masquerade as a confident final decision.

Three-part confidence

Extraction confidence, retrieval confidence, and decision confidence are stored separately. The final routing can be softened when upstream evidence is weak.

Groundedness over bravado

If policy evidence is weak or missing, the system should prefer human review or refusal rather than an unearned compliant verdict or unsupported answer.

Eval harness alignment

The evaluation harness measures verdict accuracy, retrieval quality, citation correctness, answer relevance, and refusal behavior against both seeded cases and drop-in held-out sets.
